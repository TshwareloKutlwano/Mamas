Welcome to MAMAS FOOD RESTAURANT where the warmth of home meets meets the tradition flavours,

Our story began with the simple idea: to share the love and care of streetwise/homemade food with..

our community.


Mamas food was founded by Mr Simon Ledwaba,who grew watching her mother cook up storm in the

kitchen.inspired by those childhood memories,Simon set out to create a restaurant that would create

the same kind of love and care in every bite.

reciepes to ensure every meal is delicious and satisfying,we put love into meal we serve,because we..
